    Additional Help

    Contact by telephone

    You can contact the municipality by telephone on weekdays between 8-16.30. You will be given four choices of answers:

    • Press 1 for social services, elderly care, disability and LSS.
    • Press 2 for traffic, streets, parking and construction
    • Press 3 for schools and pre-schools
    • Press 4 if you are looking for a specific person in the municipality or have other questions.

    Contact centre in Mörby Centrum:

    Danderyd municipality's contact centre is located on floor 5 in Mörby Centrum. You go here if, for example,
    you have a booked meeting with an official from the municipality, if you want to search the digital drawing archive or if you need help in a matter concerning the municipality.
